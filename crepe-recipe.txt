Ingredients:
 - 600g flour
 - 1 1/2 L milk
 - 4 spoons oil
 - 6 eggs
 
Directions:
 1. Pour flour in a large bowl. Dig a hole in the flour and add eggs in it. Mix 
    progressively eggs and flour
 
 2. Add milk in small quantities as soon as the dough become compact until there
 	is no milk left. Pour oil. Let rest for an hour.

 3. Pour a fine layer of dough in a frying pan on a high heat. Once the dough stop
 	sticking to the pan, turn it over and wait again for the other side to stop sticking
 	to the frying pan. Remove the crepe from the pan and start again until ther is no
 	dough left.
